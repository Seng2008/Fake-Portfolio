About Me

With a lifelong passion for fashion and an eye for detail, I've dedicated my career to helping others express their unique style. My approach to styling is highly collaborative, and my expertise spans a wide range of services, from personal wardrobe consultations and special occasion styling to editorial photoshoots and fashion show styling.

I'm adept at working with diverse styles, body types, and budgets, and I'm passionate about creating looks that are both on-trend and timeless. My goal is to help my clients feel confident and empowered through their clothing, ensuring they not only look their best but also feel their best.
